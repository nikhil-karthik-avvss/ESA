User Manual

This manual aims to elucidate the process and guidelines for creating, maintaining, and utilizing such a list effectively. It serves as a reference document for stakeholders involved in the admission or selection process.

Profile: "Profile" refers to the detailed information about the college or institution, including its characteristics, offerings, and any specific criteria relevant to the admission or selection process.

Rank List: "Rank list" is a compilation of candidates ranked according to their performance, typically based on predefined criteria such as marks obtained in subjects like mathematics, physics, and chemistry.

Seat Matrix: A predetermined allocation of seats in various categories or profiles based on institutional or regulatory guidelines, in the context of this manual represents the total number of seats provided by the college.

Allotment Order List: The "allotment order list" is a document detailing the students who have been successfully allotted seats in their respective colleges or institutions.
